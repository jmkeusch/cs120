Our Repertoire

We play from three main categories of music: Classical Pieces, Contemporary Instrumental, and Instrumental Pop.

The following is a sample of the pieces our musicians can play. We will work with you to curate and tailor a unique list of pieces that work best for the events, proceedings, and overall atmospheree our music can provide.

Please don't hesitate to contact us for an expanded list or request a piece you do not see listed!

  • Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart - String Quartet No. 19 in C Major, "Dissonance"
  • Franz Schubert - String Quartet No. 14 in D minor, "Death and the Maiden"
  • Joseph Haydn - String Quartet No. 62 in C Major, Op. 76, No. 3, "Emperor"
  • Antonín Dvořák - String Quartet No. 12 in F Major, Op. 96, "American"
